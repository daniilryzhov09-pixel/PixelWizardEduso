Stay Icey

Stay Icey is a fast-paced adventure where you play as a tiny ice cube in a blazing world that wants you melted. Surrounded by flames, traps, and scorching danger at every turn, you must keep your cool and outsmart the heat.

You have two goals: survive the fire… and stop it before the entire world turns to ash.
